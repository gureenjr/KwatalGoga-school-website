About Kwatal Goga International School

Building strong foundations for brighter futures.

Who We Are

Kwatal Goga International School Limited is a registered educational institution established in 2025 by the Gureen Family. Located in Bwari, Abuja, Nigeria, the school is committed to providing quality early childhood and primary education in a nurturing, secure, and well-structured learning environment.

The school is officially incorporated under the Nigerian Companies and Allied Matters Act, 2020 and is fully registered with the Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC), with the registration number RC 7639129. This affirms our commitment to excellence, compliance, and long-term educational development.

Our Mission

To deliver high-quality education that builds confident, disciplined, intelligent learners who grow into responsible members of society.

Our Vision

To become a leading educational institution known for academic excellence, moral discipline, and an environment where every child is nurtured to achieve their fullest potential.

Our Core Values

  • Discipline
  • Hard work

Our Programs

Kwatal Goga International School currently offers foundational and primary educational programs:

  • Nursery 1 — Nursery 3
  • Primary 1 — Primary 5

Future academic expansion will include:

  • Junior Secondary School (JSS1 — JSS3)
  • Senior Secondary School (SS1 — SS3)

Our Facilities

We provide dedicated facilities to enhance learning and child development:

  • Aliyu Gurin Playground — Safe outdoor play and physical development area
  • Ibrahim Gurin Library — A quiet place for study, reading, and academic exploration
  • GureenJr ICT Room — Equipped with basic ICT tools to introduce pupils to digital literacy
